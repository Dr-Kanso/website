/About LabVault

Your Comprehensive Laboratory Management Platform

LabVault is a modern, feature-rich desktop application designed to streamline laboratory workflows, enhance data management, and accelerate scientific research. Built with modern UI/UX principles and powerful backend services, it provides researchers with an all-in-one platform for laboratory management.

  • Comprehensive Dashboard & Real-time Statistics
  • Enhanced Electronic Lab Notebook (ELN)
  • 15+ Scientific Calculators & Molecular Biology Tools
  • Experiment Calendar & Project Tracking
  • Risk Assessment & COSHH Compliance
  • Google Drive Integration & Cloud Sync
  • Cross-Platform: Windows, macOS, Android, iOS
×

/Key Features

  • Comprehensive Dashboard: Real-time statistics, activity monitoring, and quick actions
  • Electronic Lab Notebook (ELN): Rich text editing, project organization, tagging, search, and cloud sync
  • Scientific Calculators & Tools: Dilutions, unit conversions, buffer prep, DNA/protein tools, and more
  • Risk Assessment & Safety: COSHH compliance, risk templates, chemical database, and SOP management
  • Experiment Management: Calendar, experiment tracking, resource planning, and timeline visualization
  • Cloud Integration: Google Drive sync and backup
  • Modern UI: Responsive, dark/light themes, accessibility, and performance optimized
